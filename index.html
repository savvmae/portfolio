ABOUT ME

Hey there, I'm a full stack JavaScript developer. I originated in Charleston, SC but I'll never turn down a good adventure, domestic or abroad. I tackle everything in life head on and without hesitation. I am a straight-shooting gal who's not afraid to roll up her sleeves and solve problems. I'm the mom of three four-legged furr chilren, one of whom is a shaved cat. I'm an expert at growing okra in the summer, but don't ask me how to organically keep the aphids at bay. I am a self-diagnosed completionist and love when life (code included) comes full circle. I possess a solution oriented mentality and am apt to find problems that need solving before they even arise; its' both a blessing and a curse. I drink more coffee than water in any given day and I have no intention of putting down the coffee cup anytime soon.

I am devoted to business cultivation and am currently looking to continue my development journey with a company where I can commit my skills to our mutual growth. I am well versed in React and popular JS libraries, but I have a hard time saying no to a little vanilla JS. I have experience building API's, Express applications and working with PSQL and Mongo. For me, the hardest part about development is deciding what to learn next.
